💡 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can a diode laser cut metal?

Most diode lasers (20W+) engrave stainless steel but cannot cut through it. To cut metal you need a fiber laser.

2. How do I price laser engraving for a client?

How to price laser engraving in 2026?

Many people starting with a diode laser or CO2 laser ask: how much should I charge per minute of laser time?

1. Hourly rate is the foundation

Professional laser engraving rates in Europe range from €15 to €50/hour depending on country and project complexity.

2. Proportional material cost

If you buy a 600×400mm plywood sheet for €6 and cut a 20×20mm project, you use just 0.17% of the sheet. Our calculator does this automatically.

3. Machine depreciation & electricity

A diode laser module has a lifespan of ~10,000 hours. Add €5–15/h as a sinking fund for servicing and electricity.

Pro tip: Always charge for file preparation time (SVG/DXF). Fixing a customer's logo often takes longer than the actual engraving!

⚠️ Laser Safety Rules

🕶️ Eye Protection

Never look at the beam without certified safety glasses (OD5+ for 450nm/1064nm). Even reflected light can permanently damage your eyes.

🚫 Forbidden Materials

Never cut PVC / vinyl (releases toxic chlorine gas). Avoid chrome-tanned leather and synthetic leather.

🔥 Supervision & Fire

Always keep a fire extinguisher or fire blanket nearby. Never leave the room while the laser is running.

❓ Troubleshooting & Tips

Common questions about laser engraving prices, materials and machine setup

How do I calculate laser engraving prices?

Add: material cost + laser time × hourly rate + design prep + machine depreciation. Market rates: €0.40–€1.10 per minute.

How much should I charge per hour?

2026 rates: Poland ~60–120 zł/h · Germany €25–45/h · UK £20–40/h · USA $25–60/h. Always add machine depreciation on top.

Best diode laser in 2026?

xTool D1 Pro 10W – best precision. Creality Falcon 2 22W – fastest. Atomstack X40 Pro 40W – most powerful.

Diode laser vs CO2 laser – which is better?

Diode lasers: cheaper (from €300), compact, great for wood and leather. CO2 lasers: cut all acrylic colors, but start at €2000+.

What materials cannot be cut with a laser?

Never cut: PVC/vinyl (toxic chlorine), chrome-tanned leather, ABS plastic, polycarbonate, carbon fiber. Always use Air Assist and ventilation.

Why are my plywood edges black/charred?

Lack of Air Assist or feed rate too slow. Also check the lens – smoke residue reduces laser power by 30–50%. Clean with isopropyl alcohol.

Best software for laser engraving?

LightBurn – industry standard, supports virtually all lasers. Free: LaserGRBL (Windows). For xTool: xTool Creative Space.
